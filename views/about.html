CHAIN

DUEL

About

Two chains duel for the next block reward. Take your friends’ hard earned sats in this revamp of the classic snake game.

Rules

  • Place your bets, play, redeem. Epoch ends when one chain hits zero sats.
  • Grow your chain by capturing UTXOs. The longer your chain the more value you capture from the rival chain.
  • If you hit a wall or your opponent you’ll reset your chain.

VALUE SPLIT

Value-for-value:
A fraction of the wagered sats is distributed on Lightning to the contributors of the project.

INFRASTRUCTURE

The architecture integrates multiple open-source projects and encourages the adoption of Bitcoin as a payment system.
